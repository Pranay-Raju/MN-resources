MN Resources Website

A premium introductory website designed to present the business professionally, build credibility, and capture enquiries.

Luxury black & gold theme Lead generation Professional brand introduction

User Persona

Business Owner

  • Wants to introduce the company professionally
  • Needs a trusted online presence
  • Wants visitors to contact the business easily
  • Needs the website to reflect quality and credibility

Business Consumer

  • Looks for suppliers in the construction or cement industry
  • Wants to understand the company quickly
  • Needs confidence in the brand before contacting
  • Prefers a clear, premium, and easy-to-navigate experience

Pain Points and Prioritization

Customer Pain Points

  • Hard to assess business credibility online
  • Limited trust without clear company information
  • Difficulty finding contact options
  • Need for a polished and premium first impression

Priority Focus

  • Build trust quickly
  • Show product and business context clearly
  • Make enquiry submission simple
  • Create a luxury brand experience

Solutions

What the website delivers

  • Premium black-and-gold styling for a luxury impression
  • Structured sections for business introduction, products, and credibility
  • Profile and product visuals to support trust
  • Enquiry form for direct lead capture

Business value

  • Improves brand perception
  • Helps visitors research the business faster
  • Encourages enquiries from serious buyers
  • Creates a modern digital introduction

User Journey

1. Visitor lands on the homepage and sees the luxury brand presentation.
2. They review the About section, products, and credibility information.
3. They gain confidence in the business profile and reputation.
4. They choose to submit an enquiry through the contact form.
5. The business receives the lead and can follow up quickly.
